Factors affecting crop production

  • Resistance to drought, flood, and salinity
  • The number of human resources available for cultivation
  • Tolerance to insect and diseases
  • Soil moisture

How to increase crop production

  • Implement soil conservation practices such as cover cropping, crop rotation, and reduced tillage to improve soil structure and fertility
  • Rotate crops and practice proper sanitation to reduce pest and disease pressure over time.
  • Monitor soil moisture levels and adjust irrigation schedules accordingly to prevent overwatering or underwatering.
  • Choose crop varieties that are suited to the local climate and weather conditions.
